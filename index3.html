About the birds

More about it

The world's rarest birds are usually found in isolated areas: the southern and northern islands, the depths of the tropics, and other areas. Their habitat is significantly limited, and the number of some species of rare birds may be only a few tens or hundreds of individuals.Currently, rare bird species are birds that permanently or temporarily live in a state of natural freedom in the territory, continental shelf and maritime economic zone. The threat to our natural world is growing every day, and many bird species are becoming extinct. Modern life is no match for them. The air is not fresh and the water is not clean. They often don't have good food or a place to live. Every day people destroy rainforests, and when trees are cut down, many birds lose their homes.
